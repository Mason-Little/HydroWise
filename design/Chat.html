Ask HydroWise

You

What’s the simplest way to understand photosynthesis vs cellular respiration? I get the words mixed up.

HydroWise

Think of them as opposite bookkeeping for energy. Photosynthesis stores energy by building sugars from sunlight + CO2. Respiration releases that energy by breaking sugars down with O2 → CO2.

Quick contrast: photo = makes fuel (glucose). Respiration = burns fuel to make ATP you can spend. They’re linked in a loop, not the same process.

Unit 2 — Energy & metabolism

Slides 4–9 · BIO 101

“Cellular respiration and photosynthesis are complementary processes: photosynthesis converts light energy to chemical energy stored in glucose; cellular respiration converts that chemical energy to ATP.”
